ABOUT US

Fun Fried Chicken, where every bite is a celebration of flavor! Founded by the passionate food enthusiast Raj Patel, our restaurant brings together the rich culinary traditions of India and the timeless comfort of fried chicken.

At Fun Fried Chicken, we believe that food should be an adventure. Our secret? A perfect blend of Indian spices that dance on your palate, elevating classic fried chicken to a whole new level. From our zesty masala marinades to our crispy, golden-brown coating, every piece of chicken is crafted with love and a sprinkle of magic.

At Fun Fried Chicken, we don’t just serve food; we serve happiness on a plate. Join us for a meal, share a laugh, and experience the joy of great company and unforgettable flavors. We can’t wait to welcome you into our family!
